title: Announcing Lift 2.3-RC5 date: 2011/03/31 10:49:42 -0700 tags: [] author: Indrajit Raychaudhuri alias: /announcing-lift-23-rc5
The Lift team is pleased to announce Lift 2.3-RC5.

This is the fifth (and possibly last) release candidate in 2.3-RC* series supporting both Scala 2.8.0 and 2.8.1. See the ticket tracker for the list of tickets closed since Lift 2.3-RC4.

All going well, we expect to ship Lift 2.3 release next week. Please test this RC one final time to help us identify bugs for Lift 2.3 final!

Thank you, have fun!
- The Lift team
