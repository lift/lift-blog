title: Announcing Lift 2.3-RC4 date: 2011/03/25 08:26:32 -0700 tags: [] author: Indrajit Raychaudhuri alias: /announcing-lift-23-rc4
The Lift team is pleased to announce Lift 2.3-RC4.

This is the fourth release candidate in 2.3-RC* series supporting both Scala 2.8.0 and 2.8.1. See the ticket tracker for the list of tickets closed since Lift 2.3-RC3.

We expect to ship Lift 2.3 release in April. Please test these 2.3-RC* series and help us identify bugs so we can make Lift 2.3 awesome!

Thank you, and have fun!
- The Lift team
